The Metro Detroit Goat People

Goat grazing is a proven method to help with vegetation management while promoting biodiversity and the growth of native plants.

Goats reduce the spread of invasive species. The seeds they eat are no longer viable once digested because of the unique enzymes in their digestive tract.

Goats reduce waste, convert foliage to fertilizer, aerate the soil, debark shrubs and branches, help control soil erosion, and cost much less than lawn clearing equipment or chemicals!
