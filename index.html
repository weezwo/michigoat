The Natural Solution for Vegetation Management and Invasive Species Control

Goat Grazing is a proven method for Vegetation Management

Goats:

  • Reduce biomass waste
  • Convert foliage to fertilizer
  • Aerate the soil and help control erosion
  • Access hilly and hard to reach terrain
  • Digestion process makes seeds unviable
  • Replace use of sprays and machines

On the other hand:

  • Herbicide use continues to have adverse effects on human health and the environment.
  • Gas powered equipment consumes fuel, emits exhaust, and increases the carbon footprint of the project.
  • Invasive species are one of the major drivers of ecosystem loss.

Goats have the ability to eat many different plants, bushes and weeds!

Goats cost less than chemicals and equipment not only for you, but also the environment!

Goats love to eat, and are lots of fun to work with!
